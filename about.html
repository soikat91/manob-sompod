About Manob Sompod

Discover our journey in human development, our commitment to community empowerment, and the dedicated team driving positive change across Bangladesh.

Who We Are

Manob Sompod was established in 2005 as a registered non-governmental organization under the NGO Affairs Bureau of Bangladesh. Based in Dhaka with field offices across 12 districts, we have been at the forefront of human resource development and community empowerment for nearly two decades.

Our organization specializes in capacity building, skills development, research, and advocacy work that addresses the root causes of social inequality. We believe in evidence-based interventions and participatory approaches that put communities at the center of their own development journey.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and communities through comprehensive human resource development programs that foster sustainable socio-economic growth, promote social justice, and create meaningful opportunities for marginalized populations across Bangladesh.

Our Vision

A just and inclusive society where every person has equal access to quality education, skill development opportunities, and the resources necessary to achieve their full potential and contribute meaningfully to national development.

Our Core Values

Excellence: Commitment to quality in all programs and services.
Inclusivity: Ensuring equal participation of all community members regardless of gender, age, or background.
Transparency: Maintaining open communication with stakeholders and accountability in all operations.
Innovation: Continuously developing creative solutions to address emerging challenges.
